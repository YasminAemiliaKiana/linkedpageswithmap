About Us

Wise Tripper was created amidst the 2020 Covid-19 pandemic, when country boarders slowly began to reopen to international travel. We are an all inclusive travel guide, informing travellers how to stay as safe as possible by providing updated travel restrictions to their chosen travel destinations. As keen travellers ourselves, we wanted to restore confidence amongst those weary of travelling by providing a place with all the information they required to take away any caution they faced prior.

We hope you have a safe trip, wise tripper.
